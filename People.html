JESU DASS MUTHU is a Professional Builder and Project Management Consultant recognized by the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB). His expertise is mainly centered in Design and Built Projects on variety of scale and specialization which includes single span structural steel buildings and building refurbishment complete with building diagnostic, rectification and retrofitting for both government and private sectors. He provides one stop technical solutions to clients in all aspects of engineering and construction projects.

JESU DASS MUTHU is a Chartered Building Engineer and his firm UBBIM RESOURCES SDN BHD provides wide range of services including Civil & Structural design, project management consultancy, value engineering, contractual & dispute resolutions and structural rehabilitation services. He is recognized by the Construction Industry Board as the pioneer in developing the National Competency Standard for Construction Project Managers in Malaysia and sits as panel member in CIDB for the certification of project managers in the country in accordance to CIDB Act 520. He had been appointed as a Panel member in various government organisations including SIRIM, Ministry of Science and Technology Malaysia and other professional organisations. He had also obtained Certificate in Adjudication and qualified to be an Adjudicator by Kuala Lumpur Centre for Arbitration (KLRCA).
