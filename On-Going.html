GUDANG B08

Project: Cadangan bagi mendirikan 1 unit bangunan gudang terbuka sisa terjadual setingkat (Gudang B08) di atas sebahagian lot 6638 di komplek pengurusan sisa terjadual (Kualiti Alam Sdn Bhd) 


Current Progress- This Project is currently on-going and commenced on the 20th of December 2020 and expected to complete on the 30th of June 2021. 

The proposed project is a steel structure warehouse to be erected on one of the Client, Kualiti Alam Sdn Bhd’s landfill ground. Being a relatively soft ground, the proposed foundation of this project is Jack- In Injection Pile with Prebore, System. Currently the total 36 numbers of 21 metres depth piles have been just completed and their pile cap works are in progress.

The infrastructure works of this project have also commenced with the drainage system completed and some of the fire protection piping laid. Soon we expect to commence the concrete road driveways together with the building concrete slab.

As soon as the current pile cap works are completed, the steel structure members, mainly portal frames with rafter structural members would be delivered to the site for erection, as they have already been prefabricated and painted at the steel factory.
